Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how information submitted on the Pyari Kunj Vrindavan landing page is handled.

Last updated: 25 April 2026.

Information we collect

If you submit the lead form on this site, we may collect your WhatsApp number along with basic visit details such as referral source, campaign tags, referrer, browser information, and the page URL used during submission.

How we use the information

We use submitted information to contact interested guests, share booking details, answer property questions, and understand which marketing channels bring visitors to the site.

Analytics and tracking

This site may use Meta Pixel, PostHog, Vercel Analytics, and Speed Insights to understand visits and improve the landing page experience.

Booking platform

Actual bookings are completed on Airbnb through the official Pyari Kunj listing. Airbnb has its own privacy policies and data handling practices.

Data sharing

Submitted information may be processed by tools used to store leads, run analytics, and manage bookings. We do not sell personal information gathered through this landing page.

Your choice

If you do not want to submit personal information, you can visit the Airbnb listing directly without filling the lead form on this site.

Contact

For booking-related communication, please use the official Airbnb listing linked from the site.
